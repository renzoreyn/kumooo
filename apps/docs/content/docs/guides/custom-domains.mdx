Free sites live at `{slug}.kumooo.site`. Custom domains are a **Pro** (and higher) feature.

## Self-host

If you deploy on your own Cloudflare account, attach a domain the usual way:

- **Workers custom domains** (recommended for one-off sites), or
- DNS routes / zone routes pointing at your Worker

That is *your* zone and *your* certificates. kumooo is not in the path.

## Hosted on kumooo (Pro+)

Customer sites on our account do **not** get a one-off Workers custom domain per brand (that does not scale). The plan is:

1. **Fallback / SaaS origin** - your site already runs as a user Worker behind `kumooo-dispatch` (Workers for Platforms).
2. **Cloudflare for SaaS (Custom Hostnames)** - you add `www.example.com` in the dashboard; we create a **Custom Hostname** on our zone and issue TLS for it.
3. **Your DNS** - you CNAME `www` (or apex via CNAME flattening / ALIAS) to our SaaS target (e.g. `sites.kumooo.dev` or similar - exact hostname ships with Pro).
4. **Routing** - the hostname maps to your site slug / Worker so the same renderer answers `example.com` and `{slug}.kumooo.site`.

So: **yes - Custom Hostnames (SSL for SaaS)**, not “add every customer domain as a Workers custom domain in wrangler.”

### Flow (when live)

1. Upgrade to Pro when billing is live (see [Pricing](https://kumooo.dev/pricing)).
2. In the dashboard, open your site → Domains.
3. Add the hostname and follow the DNS instructions (usually a CNAME to our SaaS target).
4. Wait for hostname status `active` (TXT ownership + certificate).

Until Pro billing ships, use self-host custom domains or the Free `kumooo.site` URL.
