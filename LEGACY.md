# Legacy CMS (sunset)

The Cloudflare Workers + D1 publishing CMS (`apps/api`, `apps/renderer`, `apps/dashboard`, `packages/theme-*`, `packages/db`, legacy `@kumooo/core` / `@kumooo/cli`) is **frozen**.

- No new CMS features or themes.
- Production sites may stay up during a short sunset window.
- All new product work is **kumooo.js**: Next.js App Router conventions, `@kumooo/ui`, starters, and `create-kumooo`.

See the root [README.md](./README.md) for the framework-first product.
