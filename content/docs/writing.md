This is how Kumooo should sound.

Write like a technical founder explaining something over coffee.
Confident. Relaxed. Direct. Slightly sarcastic when it helps.
Never cringe. Never arrogant.

## Rules that matter

- Short sentences. Use contractions. Prefer simple words.
- Specific beats adjectives.
- Never use em dashes.
- Don't sell. Explain. Show.

## Forbidden

Words like revolutionary, seamless, leverage, unlock, ecosystem, robust,
enterprise-grade, next-generation, world-class.

Phrases like "the future of X", "supercharge your workflow", "powered by innovation".

If it sounds like a SaaS template, delete it.

## Good examples

> Websites shouldn't need babysitting.

> Most pages are cached automatically. You don't have to think about it.

> If you enjoy configuring nginx at 2 AM, Kumooo might not be for you.

## Errors and empty states

> That login didn't work. Double-check your email and password.

> Nothing here yet. Let's fix that.

> You don't have any sites yet. Let's build one.

The best compliment is: "I forgot the CMS existed."
