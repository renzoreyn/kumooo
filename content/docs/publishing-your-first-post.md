# Your first post

You have a site. Now put words on it.

## Open the dashboard

1. Go to [dash.kumooo.dev](https://dash.kumooo.dev) and sign in.
2. Open your site.
3. Click **Posts**, then **New post**.

## Write something real

- Title: short and clear.
- Body: Markdown. Headings, lists, links, code fences all work.
- Excerpt: optional. Themes use it on list pages.

Skip the marketing fluff. Say what you mean.

## Publish

Click **Publish**. Your post goes live on `{slug}.kumooo.dev`.

If the page looks cached, wait a second and refresh. Kumooo bumps the edge cache when you publish.

## Pick a look

Open **Design / Themes**. Try Haru, Natsu, Aki, or Fuyu. Each layout is different, not just a recolor.

Want your own HTML and CSS? Use **Theme Studio**, then **Publish** there so the live site switches to `custom:{siteId}`.

## Logo and favicon

**Design / Branding**. Upload an image (counts toward your 150 MB media quota). Or leave the logo as your site title.
