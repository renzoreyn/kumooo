# Season themes

Kumooo ships four free themes named after seasons. Same platform. Different layouts.

## Live previews

Open these before you pick one in the dashboard:

- [haru.kumooo.dev](https://haru.kumooo.dev) : soft journal list
- [natsu.kumooo.dev](https://natsu.kumooo.dev) : bold summer cards
- [aki.kumooo.dev](https://aki.kumooo.dev) : chapter essays
- [fuyu.kumooo.dev](https://fuyu.kumooo.dev) : cold status log

Each site is real content on the real theme. Not a screenshot.

## What each one is for

### Haru (default)

Spring. Calm type, dated rows, gentle accent. Good default for notes and essays.

### Natsu

Summer. Stronger cards and energy. Works when posts should feel like short releases.

### Aki

Autumn. Editorial chapters. Longer reading. Drop-cap friendly prose.

### Fuyu

Winter. Minimal log. Status bar energy. Short, sharp entries.

## Light and dark

Every season respects system preference and has an on-page icon toggle (system / light / dark). Your choice sticks in `localStorage`.

## How to apply

1. Open your site in [dash.kumooo.dev](https://dash.kumooo.dev).
2. Go to **Design / Themes**.
3. Apply Haru, Natsu, Aki, or Fuyu.

`default` still works as an alias for Haru on older sites.
