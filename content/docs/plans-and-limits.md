# Plans and limits

Kumooo starts free on the managed platform. Paid tiers for more sites and storage come later. Self-host has no Kumooo site cap.

## Free plan (managed Kumooo)

| Limit | Value |
|---|---|
| Sites per workspace | **2** active |
| Media per site | 150 MB |

Archived sites do not count toward the 2. Restore fails if you are already at the cap. Delete or archive first.

## Coming later (managed)

- More sites
- Higher media quotas
- Clear paid tiers on the pricing page

## Self-host

`npx create-kumooo` with **Self-host on my Cloudflare** deploys the full multi-org stack on your account: orgs, many sites, one dashboard.

Default: `MAX_SITES_PER_ORG=0` (unlimited). Your Cloudflare bill applies. There is no Kumooo SaaS fee.
