# About Fuyu

**Fuyu** is winter mode. Cool slate, mono accents, tight chrome. Posts read like log entries or status lines. Good for ops notes, build diaries, minimal portfolios, and anyone who wants the site to feel like `tail -f` without the joke wearing thin.

Pick it when decoration is noise. You want clarity, monospace hints, and a layout that doesn't warm up the room.

Deploy a test site at [dash.kumooo.dev](https://dash.kumooo.dev) or read about Kumooo at [kumooo.dev](https://kumooo.dev).

---

This is the sparse season.

Home lists entries. Article pages strip ornament. Dark mode is the native personality; light mode stays crisp, not cozy.

Demo copy below is intentionally short. Scan it. If that pace matches how you write, keep fuyu. If you want cards or chapters, switch.

Four seasons. One renderer. Same markdown.
