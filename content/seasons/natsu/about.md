# About Natsu

Choose **natsu** when your site should hit fast. Bigger type rhythm, stronger accent, card-shaped post previews on the home page. Summer energy without turning into a landing page factory.

It works for newsletters, release notes, hot takes, and anything you'd skim on a phone at the beach. Short posts look great. Long ones still work; they just feel more direct.

Spin up a demo on [dash.kumooo.dev](https://dash.kumooo.dev) or see the platform at [kumooo.dev](https://kumooo.dev).

---

Natsu means summer. This theme is the loud cousin in the season set.

Home is a grid of cards. Title up front. Excerpt if you wrote one. The accent color does real work here: links, borders, hover states. You notice it. That's the point.

I'm not trying to build a magazine theme. I'm trying to build a theme that survives Twitter-length attention spans without dumbing down your words. Punchy titles help. So does whitespace between cards.

These four posts are sample copy. Read them fast. That's the vibe check. If you want chapter borders and drop caps, look at aki. If you want a terminal log, fuyu. If you want a journal, haru.
