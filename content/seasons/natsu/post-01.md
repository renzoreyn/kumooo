# Ship hot. Fix cool.

Natsu isn't subtle and I won't pretend it is.

You publish a post. It shows up as a card on the home page with a clear title and room to breathe. Readers tap. They read. Done.

I built this theme for people who post often and hate fiddling with layout. Your job is the headline. The theme handles contrast, focus rings, and mobile tap targets.

Summer sites shouldn't feel like homework. Keep paragraphs short. Break lines early. Link out when you mean it.

Cards also survive social previews. When someone shares your URL, the title they wrote matches the title on the page. No mismatch between Open Graph hero and actual content. Natsu keeps that honest.

If this demo feels too bright, toggle dark mode. Natsu holds up either way. The accent stays visible without glowing. Write like you're texting a smart friend, then hit publish.
