# About Aki

**Aki** is for long reads. Warm cream or ochre backgrounds, serif display type, chapter-like article rhythm. Drop caps and section borders actually have room to breathe here.

Pick it for essays, technical deep dives, interviews, and anything you'd print if printers still mattered. Reflective tone optional. Paragraph length encouraged.

Preview the theme on [dash.kumooo.dev](https://dash.kumooo.dev) or explore Kumooo at [kumooo.dev](https://kumooo.dev).

---

Autumn themes invite slow reading. Aki leans into that without feeling dusty.

The home page still lists posts, but article pages get editorial treatment: generous line height, display headings, subtle rules between sections. I wrote these demo essays long on purpose so you can see how drop caps and chapter borders behave with real text.

If natsu is a sprint, aki is a walk through leaves with a notebook. Not precious. Just paced.

Compare the four seasons side by side with the same paragraph. Haru softens it. Natsu tightens it. Fuyu strips it down. Aki lets it run.
